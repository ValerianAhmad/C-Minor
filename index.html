About Us

Serving Since 2025

Story

Founded in February 2025 by two young man and one young girl from three different area's.

C minor comes from an idea that everyone can enjoy coffee. It's all started at a small rented building on Jalan Wonocatur. By Juny 2025, C Minor has 10 branches, two of them located in Jakarta and Surabaya
